[[Games]]
**
Tags: Energizer, Reaction Speed, Physical Activity

Description: Participants stand in a circle and take turns making one quick attack movement toward another player's hand, while the defender has one chance to dodge. Players freeze after each move, and anyone whose hand is hit is eliminated until one winner remains.

Materials Needed: N/A

Time Needed: 5–10 minutes

Purpose/Benefit: Increases energy, concentration, and reaction speed while creating a fun, competitive atmosphere.
