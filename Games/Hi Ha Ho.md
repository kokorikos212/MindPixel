# Hi, Ha, Ho

## Description
Participants stand in a circle. One player points at another participant while saying **"Hi!"**. The selected participant immediately raises both hands and says **"Ha!"**. The participants standing directly to the left and right of the **"Ha!"** player simultaneously make a slicing motion toward the middle while saying **"Ho!"**. The **"Ha!"** player then points to another participant by saying **"Hi!"**, and the sequence continues as quickly as possible.

## Materials Needed
- None

## Time Needed
- 5–10 minutes

## Purpose/Benefit
- Improves reaction speed.
- Strengthens concentration.
- Enhances coordination and group synchronization.
- Creates a fun, energetic atmosphere.
