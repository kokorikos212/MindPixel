# The Wind Blows

## Description
Participants sit in a circle with one person standing in the middle. The person in the middle says, **"The wind blows for everyone who..."** followed by a characteristic (e.g., *"...likes coffee," "...is wearing black,"* or *"...has visited another country."*). Everyone matching the statement must quickly change seats while the person in the middle tries to take one of the empty seats. The participant left without a seat becomes the next caller.

## Materials Needed
- Chairs (one fewer than the number of participants)

## Time Needed
- 10–15 minutes

## Purpose/Benefit
- Helps participants discover shared interests and experiences.
- Encourages group interaction and communication.
- Keeps participants active and engaged.
- Creates a fun and energetic atmosphere.
