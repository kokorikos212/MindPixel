# Zombie Chair

## Description
Participants sit in a circle with one empty chair. One participant acts as the **Zombie** and slowly walks toward the empty chair, attempting to sit down. The seated participants must prevent this by strategically moving to the empty chair before the Zombie reaches it, continuously creating a new empty seat elsewhere in the circle. The Zombie wins if they successfully sit down.

## Materials Needed
- Chairs (one more chair than the Zombie)

## Time Needed
- 10–15 minutes

## Purpose/Benefit
- Encourages teamwork and cooperation.
- Develops communication skills.
- Promotes quick decision-making.
- Strengthens strategic thinking.
- Maintains a fun and energetic group atmosphere.
