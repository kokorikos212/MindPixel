# Hand Slap

## Description
Participants stand in a circle with one hand extended. Players take turns attempting to quickly slap the hand of the person next to them while simultaneously avoiding having their own hand slapped. Fast reactions and anticipation are key.

## Materials Needed
- None

## Time Needed
- 5–10 minutes

## Purpose/Benefit
- Develops reaction speed.
- Improves attention and concentration.
- Strengthens hand-eye coordination.
- Creates a lively and energetic atmosphere.
