# Follow the Leader

## Description
One participant leaves the room or turns away while the remaining group secretly chooses a leader. Everyone stands in a circle, and the leader begins performing simple movements that the rest of the group copies while trying not to reveal who the leader is. The outsider returns and has a limited number of guesses to identify the leader.

## Materials Needed
- None
- Music (optional)

## Time Needed
- 5–10 minutes

## Purpose/Benefit
- Develops observation skills.
- Improves concentration.
- Strengthens group coordination.
- Encourages creativity and non-verbal communication.
