# Name Toss

## Description
Participants stand in a circle and throw a soft ball to one another, saying the receiver's name before throwing. Once everyone becomes familiar with the names, additional balls can be introduced to increase the challenge and keep participants focused.

## Materials Needed
- Soft ball or beanbag

## Time Needed
- 5–10 minutes

## Purpose/Benefit
- Helps participants learn each other's names.
- Improves focus and attention.
- Encourages communication and group interaction.
- Builds confidence and familiarity within the group.
