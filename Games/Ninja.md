# Attack & Dodge

## Description
Participants stand in a circle and take turns making one quick attack movement toward another player's hand, while the defender has one chance to dodge. Players freeze after each move, and anyone whose hand is hit is eliminated until one winner remains.

## Materials Needed
- None

## Time Needed
- 5–10 minutes

## Purpose/Benefit
- Increases energy and engagement.
- Improves concentration and reaction speed.
- Encourages quick decision-making.
- Creates a fun and competitive atmosphere.
