# Storytelling with Association Cards

[[Sophie]] opened the session with an association card and storytelling exercise. Each group of four took turns contributing a single word inspired by a card, gradually weaving the words into funny and introspective stories.

- Duration: 15–20 minutes
- Effect: filled the room with laughter, helped us connect with one another

Theme: [[Connection]]
