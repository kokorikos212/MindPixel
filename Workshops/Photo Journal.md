# Photo Journal — In Pursuit of Connection

**About:** Working individually or in pairs, we set out on a walk — some through the forest, others through the city — with one simple question: *Where do we see connections?* Two cherries hanging from the same branch? An old clock that once brought a village together? Photographs of friendships and moments shared throughout the project?

**What happened:** Over ~90 minutes we captured images of nature, people, places and symbols that reflected what connection means to each of us. We paired the photographs with short texts and poems, creating a series of collages, then came back together to present them and share the stories behind them.

**The most inspiring part:** seeing how differently everyone interpreted the same prompt — everyone saw connection in different little things.

> [!quote] [[Milica]]
> The most impactful for me was photo journaling activity. The topic was connection, so it made me think about all the connection we form in Erasmus, and how that impacts our lives afterwards. It made me appreciate the passing connections even more, and it pulled me closer to myself, nature and other people.
