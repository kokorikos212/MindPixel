# Body Map & Two-Hand Dialogue

[[Luna]] invited us to reconnect with ourselves:

1. A short guided meditation.
2. Exploring emotions by representing them as colours on an outline of our bodies.
3. A dialogue between the left and right brain hemispheres — writing reflective questions with the dominant hand and answering with the non-dominant hand.
