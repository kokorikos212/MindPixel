Toolkit Creation: During this session, we worked together to create a toolkit that gathered the activities, methods, and creative ideas developed throughout the project. We organized our work into a clear and useful resource so that other young people and youth workers can easily apply these activities in future projects. We were separated into groups: designers, creators, collectors and day by day activities, we had approximately three hours to finish the assignment.
